Nirog

Nirog uses a Technology that lets you to scan any food and get the nutritional value of it on your smartphone! This innovative gadget is the first tool based on artificial intelligence that allows you to see any product scanned by its camera in milliseconds. This device scans foods from its camera as your eyes would do, with advanced Artificial Intelligence and combines this information with databases to give you results in just a second. The device has been designed for everyone, being suitable for working people, sport athletes, parents with children in emergency situations at home or out of home, as well as younger people such as students or teenagers.
